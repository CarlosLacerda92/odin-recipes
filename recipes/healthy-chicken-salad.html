Healthy Chicken Salad

Description

This healthy chicken salad recipe is made with a mixture of Greek yogurt and cottage cheese instead of regular mayonnaise, resulting in a healthier version that's still creamy and delicious but higher in protein.

Apples and dried cranberries add a sweet-tart flavor, while chopped pecans provide textural interest in every bite.

Serve it on crackers, on a croissant, in a pita, or grab a fork and just dig in!

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Cottage cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Fruit
  • Pecans
  • Seasonings
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Simply stir all the ingredients until they're well-combined
